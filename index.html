About Manattiparambu

Manattiparambu is a picturesque village nestled in the heart of Kerala, India. The name "Manattiparambu" derives from the Malayalam words "Manatti" (earthen pot) and "Parambu" (field), reflecting the village's rich tradition of pottery and agriculture.

With a population of approximately 5,000 residents, our village is a harmonious blend of traditional Kerala culture and modern development. The community is known for its warm hospitality, rich cultural heritage, and strong sense of unity.

Our village has a history spanning over 200 years, with generations of families preserving the age-old traditions of Kerala while embracing progress and education. The lush green landscapes, coconut groves, and paddy fields create a serene environment that embodies the essence of rural Kerala.

Manattiparambu is home to people from various faiths who live together in perfect harmony, celebrating festivals and traditions collectively, making it a true example of India's cultural diversity and unity.
